Frequently Asked Questions

What is Impact Signals?

Impact Signals is a daily podcast and newsletter that delivers AI-powered intelligence for people working at the intersection of technology and social impact. It covers AI for disaster relief, humanitarian operations, climate resilience, and social good.

Who is Impact Signals for?

Impact Signals is for humanitarian practitioners, social impact professionals, AI researchers, policymakers, NGO workers, and anyone interested in how artificial intelligence can be used for social good and disaster response.

How can I subscribe to Impact Signals?

You can subscribe on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube, or Substack. You can also add our RSS feed directly to your podcast player at impactsignals.ai/feed.xml.
